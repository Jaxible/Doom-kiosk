  • What is an FPS?


An FPS, or a first person shooter, is a genre of video games that focus on, well, shooting things in a first person perspective.

Players use self-guided navigation through a three-dimensional space in order to operate the game. This is different from other shooter games such as rail shooters, as they also utilize a first-person camera, but the player is unable to control how they move around in a level.

Some games, like the Portal franchise, use the first-person camera and self-guided navigation not for combat, but for other uses such as puzzles.

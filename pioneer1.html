^ A Deathmatch ^

The original Doom pioneered many aspects of modern video games as we know them now, such as....

Deathmatches:

Friends and coworkers could play together in LAN parties (basically a bunch of people with a bunch of computers in a room connected to the same server; the Xbox Live parties before Xbox Live) in multiplayer where they could compete for the most kills. Some businesses actually banned Doom from being played at the office due to workers using the computers to compete in deathmatches rather than working!

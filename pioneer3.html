Modding:

Doom was built from the ground up with the idea of having users be able to create and share their own custom levels and enemies. These mods, or as they were called then, WADs (short for Where's All the Data files) could be as simple as a new map, new weapons, or even entirely new games based on the Doom engine. There are likely tens of thousands of custom WAD files in existence across multiple different sites, and those are just the public ones.

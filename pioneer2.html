^ Walkable Stairs ^
Verticality:

Most first-person games before Doom could only run on one plane; no stairs, platforms, or any changes in height at all. With Doom, not only could the player move from floor to floor, but enemies could exist on different levels as well! However, since the different heights was only really possible through graphical programming trickery, no walkable space could be placed directly over another.
